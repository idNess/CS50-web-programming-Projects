Fancy Lemons

Fancy Lemons is a company that specializes in the production and sale of fancy colored citrus fruit. In addition to Fancy Lemons, we produce and sell classic yellow citrons, bergamot, Messina IGP lemons and other varieties. The wide range of fruits produced allows the company to offer its customers excellent citrus fruit all year round. Fancy Lemons are special citrus fruits with a natural color on the inside. Fancy Lemons differ from the other citrus fruit also for a very intense taste in the pulp and for their abundant juice. The peel is light yellow, of middle thickness, fine grained. The maturation of the fruit begins at the end of October and is extended into the first days of April.

Our secret

Fancy Lemons are Biologic and GMO-Free. Believe it or not, the pigmentation is 100% natural and it is due to a secret formula that our grand-father invented in 1901. We can offer you 8 different varieties that only differ in the pigmantation... the taste is always awesome!
